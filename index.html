Max's Guessing Game

This is my guessing game that I've created for my Code 201 class. It includes a greeting prompt, a variety of yes or no questions about me, a game in-which you try to guess my favorite number, as well as one more fun guessing question.

1. Was I born in the state of Iowa? (yes or no)

2. Is baseball my favorite sport? (yes or no)

3. Is pizza my favorite food? (yes or no)

4. A guessing game where you try to find my favorite number between 1-50.

5. Is Appa the coolest flying bison in existence? (yes or no)

6. How many licks does it officially take to get to the center of a tootsie pop? (enter a number)
